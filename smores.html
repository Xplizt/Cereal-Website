Almost S'mores

5 Minutes |

1 Servings

Ingridients

• 3/4 cup VKellogg’s Frosted Flakes cereal
• 1/2 cup milk, vanilla almond milk, soy milk or chocolate milk
• 1 tablespoon semi-sweet chocolate morsels or dark chocolate morsels
• 1 tablespoon miniature marshmallows

Directions

1. Pour VKELLOGG’S FROSTED FLAKES cereal into serving bowl. Add milk to cereal. Sprinkle with chocolate morsels and marshmallows. Serve immediately.
