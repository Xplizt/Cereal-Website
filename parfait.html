Cocoa Yogurt Parfait

10 Minutes |

2 Servings

Ingridients

• 1 container (6 oz.) low-fat raspberry, lemon, orange or vanilla yogurt
• 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
• 1/4 cup frozen reduced-fat non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
• 1/3 cup VKellogg's Cocoa Krispies cereal
• 1 tablespoon flaked coconut, toasted
• 1 cup fresh raspberries, sliced strawberries or cut-up mango

Directions

1. In small bowl stir together yogurt and vanilla. Fold in whipped topping. In another small bowl stir together VKELLOGG’S COCOA KRISPIES cereal and coconut. Set aside.

2. In two parfait glasses or dessert dishes alternate layers of yogurt mixture, raspberries and cereal mixture.
