About Us

At VKellogg's, we bring our best to everyone, every day through our trusted foods and brands.

VKellogg’s, a name synonymous with breakfast foods and snacks, traces its roots back to 1906 when our founder, Will Keith Kellogg, revolutionized the way people started their day.

Established in Battle Creek, Michigan, VKellogg’s began its journey with a simple yet innovative idea – to create a nutritious and delicious cereal that would make breakfast more enjoyable and accessible for everyone. Over the decades, VKellogg’s has grown to become a household name, known globally for its commitment to quality and innovation in food products.

Our Mission

At VKellogg’s, our mission is to provide nourishing and delicious food that brings smiles to every table.

We believe in the power of breakfast to energize and fuel the day, and our diverse range of products is crafted with care to meet the nutritional needs and taste preferences of our customers around the world. We are dedicated to enhancing the lives of our consumers by offering food products that combine the best of taste, nutrition, and convenience.
